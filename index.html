About

The art of stained glass brings light and colour together in an exceptional and almost magical way. Judy B Stained Glass utilises highly skilled techniques to create contemporary Tiffany style stained glass pieces. Each individual piece of glass is hand-cut and then wrapped in copper foil so that they can be soldered together and assembled. This copper foil technique was developed by Tiffany Studios in the 1890s and allows for small pieces of glass to be joined together to create more intricate designs.

Background

Judy B Stained Glass has almost 30 years of experience in designing, making and repairing stained glass pieces. Judy B started creating stained glass as a hobby and since then has developed her passion into a business. Judy B enjoys choosing the glass, seeing how it works with the light and then creating something unique.
