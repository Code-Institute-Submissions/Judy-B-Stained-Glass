Welcome

Judy B Stained Glass provides a modern twist to an age old artform creating custom handcrafted stained glass pieces from lamps to wall art and even some eye-catching pieces for the garden.

About

The art of stained glass brings light and colour together in an exceptional and almost magical way. Judy B Stained Glass utilises highly skilled techniques to create contemporary Tiffany style stained glass pieces.

Background

Judy B Stained Glass has been in business for 28 years from what started out as a hobby. The distinctive effect of stained glass is sure to enhance any home.
